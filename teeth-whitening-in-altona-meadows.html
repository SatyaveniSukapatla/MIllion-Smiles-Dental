    • Teeth whitening often referred to as bleaching, is a highly effective and affordable procedure that whitens teeth and leaves them feeling ultra-clean. There are many factors that can contribute to stained or yellowed teeth such as acidic foods, dark liquids (tea, coffee, red wine and juices), smoking, certain prescription medications, a lack of proper oral hygiene, the ageing process, trauma and even genetics. A combination of these factors over a lifetime can cause your teeth to lose their sparkle.

    Having teeth stains not only impacts your appearance, it can also diminish your self-confidence. A bright, white smile can help improve your success at work and in your personal life since smiles are one of the first things that people notice when meeting someone new.

    A study commissioned by a major oral health company called “The Impact of Whiter Teeth on Key First Impressions” shows that personal appearance is seen as an indicator of personal and financial success. Luckily, teeth whitening is one of the simplest and most affordable ways to give you a brighter, whiter smile right away.
